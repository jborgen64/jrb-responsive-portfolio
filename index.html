About Me

I graduated from University of Missouri in 2014 with a degree in Hospitality. I have since had a career managing various facets of the Kansas City Marriott Downtown, most recently in their Food and Beverage division.

I recently decided to look into a complete career change and Computer Programming has always been of great interest to me. The rest is history as I am currently working towards completing KU's Coding Bootcamp Program.

Aside from my professional interests I enjoy all things cooking and at one point was interested in becoming a chef and still have aspirations to own a restaurant. I also enjoy music and play several string instruments. In my free time I lounge around with my fiance and boxer puppy child whenever I can.
